An Ominous OMEN

TL;DR A collaboration with my friend Mathieu where we finetune a language model on his YouTube persona. 		Updated 2022-01-22
Author Jan Kirchner

My good friend Mathieu has this YouTube channel where he makes science communication videos about (mostly) Marine biology. I admire the care he puts into each and every video of his, so when he asked if I wanted to collaborate on one I didn’t hesitate.

There isn’t a lot of technical novelty beyond what I already did with IAN, but it’s definitely fun to see what a YouTube creator can do with a fine-tuned language model.

Also, what a visual firework!
